Tunnel

Android Game Development Unity C# Game Design

Tunnel was the second game I published on the playstore. You control a small player (triangle) and try to evade a series of obstacles which come at you from ahead (similar to life?). I wanted to go for something trippy with a nice beat. It was because of those factors that playtesting was an incredibly fun experience. I thought it would be amazing to let other people try and beat my score, so far none have. Why don't you give it a try?

Square Ninja

Android Game Development Unity C# Game Design

Square Ninja was the first game project I was able to complete. You take control of a square, and you are defending your kind (The Squares) in a 42 year old war between the squares and the triangles(Yes, I am serious). Do you have what it takes to survive the onslaught of the triangle army? Its funny how lack of graphics can form the premise for your game. Honestly I love the name and the background story behind it. If you enjoy dashing through enemies with cool slow motion effects this is the game for you. You could even try beating the high score on the leaderboard, even I'm second at the moment!
